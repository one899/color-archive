🍽️ OS16 Flashing Guide

Three flashing methods, choose as needed

🔧 Method 1: Flash via Toolbox

Requirements A computer (try to disable antivirus), a phone, system package, USB cable

📋 Steps:

  1. After downloading the system package, extract it.
  2. Open 可怜太可怜's Toolbox on your computer (password available in his group), disable antivirus software.
  3. In the toolbox, select option 1 or 3, then choose whether to watch the tutorial.
  4. Select the extracted folder (the toolbox will auto-extract), connect your phone via USB in advance.
  5. Choose whether to wipe data based on your situation.

📲 Method 2: TWRP/OrangeFox / ADB Sideload

Requirements A computer, a phone, TWRP/OrangeFox img, USB cable, system package, Toolbox (e.g., 搞机助手)

📋 Steps:

  1. Connect your phone via USB, open 搞机助手.
  2. Once detected, reboot your phone from system to bootloader mode.
  3. In the toolbox, switch to the bootloader tab and select temporary flash recovery.
  4. After entering recovery, there are two ways to flash:

🔹 Option A (ADB Sideload):

  1. On your computer, click the recovery mode tab and enter sideload mode.
  2. On your phone, go to Advanced → tap ADB Sideload, swipe the slider, and the system will flash automatically.

🔸 Option B (Flash from phone storage):

  1. Place the system package in your phone storage.
  2. Go to the Install tab, select the system package, and swipe the slider.
  3. Reboot your system.
⚠️ Note: Some devices may encounter errors. If so, switch to Method 1.
⚠️ It is not recommended to use Method 2 on ColorOS 15.0.0.400 (may cause minor bugs).

🔄 Method 3: Upgrade from OxygenOS 15 to 16

Requirements System package, phone

Prerequisite: Your phone must be running OxygenOS 15.0.0.703 or higher.

📋 Steps:

  1. Go to Settings → System Update.
  2. Tap the three dots in the top-right corner and select Local Install.
  3. Select the system package and tap Install Now.
💡 Tip: If you don't see the "Local Install" option, enable Developer Options and it will appear.
