❓ FAQ
🔧 Does installing Color Archive remove cloud control?
ColorOS's cloud control strategy is centralized yet scattered. Cannot modify for specific needs; keeping it helps with future features.
📦 Upload requirements? Can TWRP flash it?
No. Requires a specific method to flash.
⏳ How long until I receive the build? Where?
Time varies. Will be sent to the email you provided. Please don't ask frequently.
🔄 Will I get free updates later?
Yes. You need to re-onboard for each minor version update (e.g., 16.0.1 → 16.0.3).
📱 Can I relock BootLoader after flashing?
No, Color Archive is a third-party system.
🆙 Received official OTA after flashing, can I upgrade?
Yes.
⚠️ Flashing Failed?
Why can't I boot normally?
Possible causes: network issues, insufficient storage, corrupted system files, software conflicts. Check community discussions or official docs.
